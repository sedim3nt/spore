# Follow-Up Email Templates

Send within 24 hours of any meeting. Speed signals professionalism and seriousness.

---

## Template 1 — Standard Follow-Up

```
Subject: Following up — [Meeting Topic]

Hi [Name],

Thanks for the time today. A few notes from our conversation:

- [Key thing discussed or agreed to]
- [Any commitment you made]
- [Any commitment they made]

[Specific next step]: I'll [send / prepare / connect] by [date].

Looking forward to it.

[Your name]
```

---

## Template 2 — With Clear Next Step / Action Items

```
Subject: Next steps from our call

Hi [Name],

Good conversation. Here's where we landed:

My action items:
- [ ] [Thing you're doing] — by [date]
- [ ] [Thing you're doing] — by [date]

Yours (please confirm):
- [ ] [What they agreed to] — by [date]

[Any other relevant note — decision made, thing to share, etc.]

Let me know if I missed anything.

[Your name]
```

---

## Template 3 — Resource / Material Follow-Through

```
Subject: Resources from our conversation

Hi [Name],

As promised — [the thing you said you'd share]:

- [Link / document / tool]
- [Brief explanation of what it is and why it's relevant]

[Optional: one-line observation relevant to them specifically]

Let me know if you have questions.

[Your name]
```

---

## Template 4 — Warm Introduction Follow-Up

```
Subject: Great to connect

Hi [Name],

Really enjoyed talking with [mutual connection who introduced you] in mind — 
the conversation went [where it went].

I'll [specific thing] as discussed. Looking forward to [the next thing].

Best,
[Your name]
```

---

## Template 5 — No Clear Outcome, Keeping Door Open

```
Subject: Good to connect

Hi [Name],

Thanks for making time today. I found our conversation about [topic] genuinely interesting — 
especially [specific thing they said that resonated].

No immediate asks — I just wanted to say thank you and keep the connection warm.

If [relevant scenario] ever becomes relevant for you, I'd love to be a resource.

[Your name]
```

---

## Writing a Custom Follow-Up with Your Agent

```
Write a follow-up email to [Name] at [Company].

Meeting context: [What we discussed]
My commitments: [What I said I'd do]
Their commitments: [What they said they'd do]
Next step: [What happens next]
Tone: [Professional/warm, brief — keep under 100 words]
```

---

## Timing Guide

| Meeting Type | Follow-Up Window |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Sales / partnership | Within 2 hours |
| Job interview | Within 4 hours |
| Investor meeting | Within 1 hour |
| Client check-in | Within 24 hours |
| Networking / coffee | Within 48 hours |
| Conference meeting | Before conference ends |
