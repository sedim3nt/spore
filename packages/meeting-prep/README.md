# Meeting Prep Agent

**Version:** 1.0 | **Setup Time:** 5 minutes

Research any person or company before a meeting in 10 minutes. Get a structured brief with background, key topics, talking points, and ready-to-send follow-up email.

## Quick Start

1. Copy ROLE.md to your agents directory
2. Copy templates to your workspace
3. Before a meeting: "Prep me for a meeting with [Name] from [Company] on [topic]"
4. Agent returns a structured brief in ~5 minutes

## What This Package Does

- Research individuals: background, current role, public writing, social presence
- Research companies: size, funding, products, recent news, competitive position
- Generate meeting briefs with suggested talking points
- Draft follow-up emails after meetings

## Files Included

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| ROLE.md | Agent role and research process |
| templates/person-research.md | Individual research template |
| templates/company-research.md | Company research template |
| templates/meeting-brief.md | Pre-meeting brief format |
| templates/follow-up-email.md | Post-meeting follow-up drafts |
