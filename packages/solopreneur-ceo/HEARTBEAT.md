# HEARTBEAT.md

_Simplified for solo operators — 3 check-in points per day, email triage, and weekly review._

## Heartbeat Schedule

<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Adjust times to your actual work rhythm -->

- Send one concise check-in **3× per day**
- Target times: 08:00, 13:00, 18:00 (your timezone)
- Keep it under 5 lines unless something is wrong

## What Each Heartbeat Includes

**Morning (08:00)**
- Any overnight email worth flagging
- Today's priority (from your last memory log)
- One suggested focus for the day

**Midday (13:00)**
- Status on any in-progress tasks
- Any blockers detected
- Nothing if all clear

**Evening (18:00)**
- Day summary (2-3 sentences)
- Anything to carry forward to tomorrow
- Daily memory log written to `memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md`

## Critical Blocker Override

Alert immediately if:
- Security or privacy incident
- Email that needs a same-day response
- Any service failure affecting your work
- Something time-sensitive you might miss

## Email Triage Rules

<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Add your email triage preferences -->

- Flag anything from: [your key contacts]
- Deprioritize: newsletters, automated notifications
- Draft a response if: the answer is clear and low-stakes
- Ask before replying to: anything that requires your voice or judgment

## Weekly Review (Sunday or Monday)

Every week, produce:
1. What shipped (projects completed or advanced)
2. What stalled (and why)
3. Priority for the coming week
4. Anything to add to long-term MEMORY.md
