# Editorial Calendar

Track your content pipeline from idea to published across all platforms.

## How to Use

1. Add ideas to **Backlog** as they come
2. Move to **This Week** when you're committed to writing it
3. Move to **In Progress** when drafting starts
4. Move to **Review** when draft is complete
5. Move to **Scheduled** when publication date is set
6. Move to **Published** with the URL
