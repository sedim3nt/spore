# Practice Exercises Template

Active practice produces learning. Passive reading produces the illusion of learning.

## Exercise Design Principles

**Good exercises:**
- Require you to produce something (code, explanation, design, decision)
- Have a verifiable outcome (you know if you got it right)
- Expose gaps in understanding (if it's too easy, it's not working)
- Build toward the actual skill you want

**Bad exercises:**
- Re-reading notes
- Watching another video
- "Think about" or "consider" prompts with no output
- Fill-in-the-blank where the answer is obvious
