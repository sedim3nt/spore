# Content Quality Standard

The 10-point check. Every piece passes all 10 before leaving the workspace.

## The 10 Points

### 1. Clear thesis
Can you state the piece's point in one sentence? If not, rewrite.

### 2. Headline delivers
The headline makes a promise. The body cashes it. No bait-and-switch.

### 3. Strong opening
First 2 sentences earn the next 5 minutes. No wind-ups. No "In today's world..."

### 4. Specific throughout
Numbers beat adjectives. Examples beat principles. "40% faster" beats "significantly faster."

### 5. Consistent voice
One voice, start to finish. No tonal whiplash between sections.

### 6. De-AI-ified
Zero AI tells. See `de-ai-ify-wordlist.md`. If you find any: rewrite, don't just delete.

### 7. "So what?" test
Every section passes: why does this matter? Who cares?

### 8. Purposeful ending
Ends with force, a call to action, or a genuine open question. Not a summary.

### 9. Clean formatting
Paragraphs ≤ 4 sentences. Headers logical and scannable. No walls of text.

### 10. Platform-appropriate
Correct length, format, tone for target platform. See `templates/platform-adaptation.md`.

---

**9 out of 10 is not a pass. All 10.**
