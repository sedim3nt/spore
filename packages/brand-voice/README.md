# Brand Voice Agent

**Version:** 1.0 | **Setup Time:** 20 minutes

Build and enforce a distinctive brand voice across all your content. Includes a process to extract your authentic writing style from 10 samples, a de-AI-ification wordlist, and platform adaptation guides.

## Quick Start

1. Copy files to your workspace
2. Gather 10 examples of your best writing (blog posts, emails, social posts)
3. Follow `voice-profile-builder.md` to analyze them
4. Fill in your voice profile in ROLE.md
5. Test: "Rewrite this paragraph in my brand voice: [paste text]"

## What This Package Does

- Extract your authentic voice from writing samples
- Strip AI tells from generated content
- Adapt voice appropriately for each platform
- Enforce quality standard across all content

## Files Included

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| ROLE.md | Brand voice agent role |
| CONTENT-STANDARD.md | 10-point quality checklist |
| voice-profile-builder.md | Extract your voice from samples |
| de-ai-ify-wordlist.md | Words/phrases to strip |
| templates/platform-adaptation.md | Voice adjustments by platform |
